AEC HOSTEL 8 APPLICATION
INSTRUCTIONS


REGISTRATION

  1. Install the app from the link click here.
  2. Register your profile by going to the Register Section.

TO GIVE ATTENDANCE

  1. Open the ATTENDANCE APP and enter login details.
  2. Allow Location Access (Location Access is a must as attendance can be given only from AEC Girls Hostel).
  3. Click the black box in the centre. Your camera should open.
  4. Click a picture and press the submit button to enter your attendance.

TO EDIT PROFILE

Once registered, you cannot change your PROFILE details unless you get permission from the Hostel Warden. Contact your Hostel Warden to get permission.

Once you get permission, here are the steps :
  1. Click on the Pencil icon near your name in the Dashboard.
  2. Click on EDIT PROFILE on the top left corner.
  3. Change the details.
  4. Click on the UPDATE button at the bottom of the form.
